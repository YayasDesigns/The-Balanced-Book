Important Notes & Caveats

  • Conservative estimates: These projections assume steady organic growth with modest paid marketing. Viral social media moments or press coverage could accelerate growth significantly.
  • POD margins are thin: At $23.05 print cost (Lulu), margins during months 1–9 are tight. The real profit comes after switching to bulk printing at ~$3.50/unit.
  • Month 10 dip: The one-time $1,750 bulk order investment causes a temporary loss. This pays for itself within 1 month of bulk-rate sales.
  • Platform fees: Estimated at 5% average across Etsy (~6.5%), Shopify (~2.9%), and direct sales (payment processing only ~2.9%).
  • Shipping: Not itemized separately — assumed either included in price or passed to customer. Lulu POD handles fulfillment; bulk orders need self-fulfillment or 3PL.
  • Wholesale not included: Year 2 projections are direct-to-consumer only. Wholesale (at $17.50/unit) would increase volume but lower per-unit profit.
  • Taxes not included: All figures are pre-tax. Set aside ~25–30% of net profit for self-employment and income taxes.
  • No salary included: These profits do not account for paying yourself. Consider that a separate line item once profits are consistent.
