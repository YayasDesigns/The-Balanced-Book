Clear Space & Rules

Minimum clear space around the wordmark equals the cap height of "B" in "Balanced" (marked as "B" below). No other elements should enter this zone.

The Balanced Book

Rules

Do
  • ✓ Maintain clear space on all sides
  • ✓ Use approved color combinations only
  • ✓ Use monogram when space is under 60px wide
  • ✓ Center-align for stacked, left-align for inline
Don't
  • ✗ Stretch or compress the wordmark
  • ✗ Change the font or letter spacing
  • ✗ Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • ✗ Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
