Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Balanced Book.

It's a hybrid planner and journal that combines daily productivity tools (tasks, goals, habit tracking) with mindfulness features (gratitude, affirmations, mood tracking, reflections). Think of it as your single companion for both getting things done and checking in with yourself.
The Balanced Book is completely undated. You can start using it any day of the year — no wasted pages, no pressure to begin on January 1st.
Each book covers approximately 2 months of daily use. With ~140 pages including monthly overviews, weekly spreads, and daily pages, every day gets a full two-page spread for planning, gratitude, reflections, and wellness tracking. That depth is what makes this planner different — and it keeps the book compact enough to carry everywhere.
A5 size (5.8 × 8.3 inches / 148 × 210 mm). It's the sweet spot — large enough to write comfortably, small enough to slip into a bag or backpack.
S.A.R.E. stands for Sleep, Appetite, Recreation, and Energy. It's a simple daily wellness snapshot — you rate each category on a 1–10 scale using a bar chart. Over time, you'll spot patterns in your well-being that help you make better choices.
The book uses 120gsm dot-grid paper, which is pen-friendly and resists bleed-through from most pens and markers. We recommend ballpoint or fine-tip pens for the best experience. Heavy markers or brush pens may show some ghosting.
Yes! The spiral (wire-o) binding means the book lays completely flat on your desk, and you can fold it back on itself if you prefer writing on one side at a time.
We ship through our print-on-demand partners, who offer worldwide shipping. Exact shipping costs and delivery times depend on your location and will be shown at checkout.
